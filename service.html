One-Stop Service For Products

Product R&D

We always conduct multi research on product development based on customers’ needs. Research includes formula estimation, taste analysis, packaging material analysis, raw material analysis, production process research and formulation-making etc.

Manufacturing

We start from small batch production based on a determined production process and quality control program. Once the small-batch quality passes, formal production is arranged according to the scheduled delivery time.

Quality Control

We select factories with the highest responsibility for quality—and participate throughout the process to ensure every batch meets your standards.

Logistics & Export Service

We plan transportation to prevent damage in transit, and handle export services—commodity inspection, customs declaration, shipping, and all overseas logistics.
