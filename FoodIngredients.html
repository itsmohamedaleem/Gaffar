Savoury Basis

Savoury Basis is a premium, clean-label flavor base made from just gluten-free fermented soy sauce, maltodextrin, and salt.

Our meticulous spray-drying process locks in the rich umami of traditional soy sauce, yielding a light-brown, free-flowing powder with consistent taste, aroma, and color.

Perfect for dry seasoning blends, snack coatings, sauces, meat marinades, soups, and ready meals. Its minimal ingredient list and allergen-friendly profile make it ideal for clean-label and gluten-sensitive formulations.
