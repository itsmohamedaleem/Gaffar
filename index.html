About Us

Infinife Proteins is located in Pernambut, Tamil Nadu, India, with 20+ years of experience in supplying food & pet food ingredients, seasonings, and packaged foods.

We provide complete solutions – from product development and quality checks to logistics and export. Our expert team brings deep industry knowledge and responds to customer needs quickly and professionally.

At Infinife, customers always come first. We follow a simple, fast, and efficient system to improve communication and service. With trust, quality, and confidentiality at our core, we’ve been a reliable partner for clients in India and across the world.
